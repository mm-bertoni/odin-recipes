Chicken Stir-Fry

Servings: 4

Calories: 366

Category: Meal

Cuisine: Asian

Equipment

  • Small Bowl
  • Large Skillet

Ingredients

  • 0.5 Cup Reduced Sodium Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Honey
  • 2 Tsp Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Canola Oil
  • 1 Head Broccoli Cut cut into Florets
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into 1" pieces
  • Fresh ground black pepper
  • Optional: Rice

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil.
  2. In a large skillet over high heat, heat oil. Cook broccoli, and garlic, stirring frequently, until softened, about 5 minutes. Add chicken and cook, tossing occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through, about 8 minutes. Stir in cashews; season with pepper.
  3. Pour sauce into skillet and bring to a simmer. Cook, stirring occasionally, until thickened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Optional: Serve over rice
